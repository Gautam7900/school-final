Our History

29 Years of Shaping Futures

BrightMind School was established in 1995 by Padma Shri Dr. Arvind Mehta with a vision to provide quality education that blends academic rigour with character development.

From a modest beginning of 80 students and 8 teachers, we have grown into a premier institution with over 2,500 students, 120+ qualified teachers, and state-of-the-art infrastructure spanning 5 acres.
